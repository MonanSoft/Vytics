    Settings

    Vytics allows you to do your own customization in many different ways. To customize Vytics, follow steps below.

    1. Click on settings icon on the top right corner (wrench and hammer icon) and then select Application Settings.

    2. The settings view will display. You will see settings groups on the left hand side and a list of settings corresponding to the selected group on the right hand side.
